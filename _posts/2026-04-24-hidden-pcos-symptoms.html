Introduction to PCOS

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects millions of women worldwide. The condition is characterized by a range of symptoms, including irregular periods, weight gain, and acne. However, there are many PCOS symptoms that nobody talks about, and it's essential to raise awareness about these hidden symptoms to help women better understand their condition.

Commonly Overlooked PCOS Symptoms

Some of the lesser-known PCOS symptoms include: * Mental health issues, such as depression and anxiety * Digestive problems, like bloating and constipation * Skin issues, including acne, eczema, and psoriasis * Hair loss and thinning * Sleep apnea and insomnia These symptoms can significantly impact a woman's quality of life, making it crucial to address them through a combination of medical treatment and lifestyle changes.
